Play game

After registering in the application, you click on start, a new window will be shown.

It takes place in a 7x7 board, every square can be occupied by characters, except for a few selected ones

5 randomly generated invaders will be shown at the start and another 5 will be shown for your army over 10 rounds.

The player must assign them to form groups (colonies) of the same type of invader in order to get points.

Game Over:

  • When a colony of 5 or more leader type characters
  • The last turn (the 10th) is reached and the board has some free space
  • When all the squares on the board are occupied by an invader and there is no possibility to place more
